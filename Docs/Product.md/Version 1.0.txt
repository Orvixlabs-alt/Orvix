# Product

ORVIX is an AI Workforce Platform where businesses hire AI Employees that don't just answer questions—they understand business knowledge, collaborate, and help complete real work.

---

# Mission

Build intelligent AI Employees that make businesses faster, smarter, and more productive.

---

# Vision

Every business should have access to an intelligent AI workforce.

---

# Problem

Businesses use too many disconnected tools.

Most AI products provide answers but don't actually help complete business work.

Teams waste time switching between software, searching for information, and repeating the same tasks.

---

# Solution

ORVIX provides AI Employees that understand a company's knowledge, work together, and help execute business tasks from one intelligent workspace.
