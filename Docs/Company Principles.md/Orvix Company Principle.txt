# ORVIX Company Principles

## 1. Users First
Every feature must solve a real problem.

## 2. Build Small
Launch simple. Improve continuously.

## 3. Quality > Quantity
One excellent feature is better than ten average ones.

## 4. Engineering First
Clean, scalable and maintainable code.

## 5. Long-Term Thinking
Build for years, not weeks.

## 6. Learn Every Day
Always improve.

## 7. Stay Humble
Let the product speak louder than marketing.
